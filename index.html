About Me

Welcome to my Website, I'm Ben Blanj! I'm a User Experience (UX) designer based in Long Island. With a background in fine arts, I bring a unique blend of creativity and analytical thinking to my design process. I have a strong interest in motion design, which allows me to incorporate dynamic and engaging elements into my UX work. I strive to be a do-er, so I designed and coded my own portfolio website. There's a great deal of competition in this field, and my goal is to become the designer who evolves with this industry by utilizing my versatility and innovation.
"I fight for the users" - Tron

Projects

During my time in the Columbia Engineering UX/UI bootcamp, I had the opportunity to collaborate with diverse teams and tackle real-world UX/UI challenges.

Puppet Works Brooklyn

This non-profit serves the community by performing puppet show for children, and by keeping the arts in Brooklyn alive. They have a legacy spanning over 3 decades, but their website was missing essential features, and is a perfect candidate for a "level-lution" design overhaul.

Redesigning Nahow.de

The primary objective for Nahow.de and other non-profits is give users all the neccesary information about their mission, and to turn site traffic into donations. My team and I uncovered insights about user expectations, trustworthiness, and perceptions of non-profit organizations.
